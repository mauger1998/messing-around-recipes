English Chicken Curry

About this dish

This isn't your typical "knock your socks off hot" chicken curry, this is different. I'm not a massive fan of spicey myself so this curry contains sweeter elements such as coconut and mango chutney.

This is a quick dish perfect for yourself or for the entire family!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 10g dessicated coconut
  • 2tsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1/2tbsp fresh ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2-3tsp curry powder
  • 1/2tsp cumin seeds
  • 1tbsp flour
  • 1tbsp tomatoe puree
  • 1tsp tumeric, ground
  • 1/2tsp cinnamon
  • 2tbsp mango chutney
  • 1/2 pint chicken stock
  • squeeze lemon juice
