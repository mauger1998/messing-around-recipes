English Chicken Curry

About this dish

This isn't your typical "knock your socks off hot" chicken curry, this is different. I'm not a massive fan of spicey myself so this curry contains sweeter elements such as coconut and mango chutney.

This is a quick dish perfect for yourself or for the entire family!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken thighs
  • 10g dessicated coconut
  • 2tsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1/2tbsp fresh ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2-3tsp curry powder
  • 1/2tsp cumin seeds
  • 1tbsp flour
  • 1tbsp tomato puree
  • 1tsp tumeric, ground
  • 1/2tsp cinnamon
  • 2tbsp mango chutney
  • 1/2 pint chicken stock
  • squeeze lemon juice

Steps

  1. Place chciken thighs in ovenproof dish and cook for 1/2 hour
  2. Preheat oven to 180c
  3. Put coconut into a bowl, cover with hot water and put to one side
  4. Put the onion in a saucepan with 2 tsp oil, add the ginger and garlic and fry over a low heat for 5 mins
  5. Stir in the curry powder, cinnamon and cumin seeds. Fry for 1 min. Add the flour, tomato puree and squeeze lemon juice then add the cocnut and water
  6. Add all remaining ingredients, bring to the boil stirring then lower the heat and simmer for 20 mins
  7. Remove the chicken from the oven and take off the bone. Add to the curry.
  8. Serve with rice
